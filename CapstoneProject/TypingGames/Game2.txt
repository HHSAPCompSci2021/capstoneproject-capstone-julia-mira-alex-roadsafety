The Mona Lisa, painted by Leonardo da Vinci, is one of the most famous art pieces in all of history. Art appreciators from around the world have been mesmerized by the beauty of the painting. She is famous because of many different reasons, but once of them is the unique characteristics of her smile. Using shades and soft sfumato at the edges of her mouth, viewing the Mona Lisa from different angles will make her smile look different. In 1962, the Mona Lisa was evaluated to be 100 million USD! In today's money, that is 900 million, almost 1 billion dollars.
